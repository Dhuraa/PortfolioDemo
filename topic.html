My Hobbies

Cooking

Ever since I was a child, I was a foodie ! Everytime i ate outside, I always get curious as to how its made, and to replicate it ! And hence my love for cooking started ! I find it as a great way to explore cultures. Trying new foods from different cultures can lead you to find new favorite dishes, but it can also lead you to a deeper appreciation of the culture and the people. You can expand your horizons and taste the world from the comfort of your own kitchen. I believe cooking is a versatile hobby. You can listen to music and dance around your kitchen as you prepare dinner. You can spend quality time with others while cooking, too. For me cooking is a type of meditation and I get great satisfaction from cooking tatsy food !
